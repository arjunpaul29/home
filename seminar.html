We define the notions of pure sheaves, torsion filtrations, E-regular divisors and prove that the E-regular divisors forms an open dense subset of the linear system $|L|$. Then we derive an expression of the Hilbert polynomial \[P(E,m) = \sum\limits_{i=0}^d \chi(E|_{\mathop\cap\limits_{j \leq i}H_j}) {{m+i-1}\choose{i}} = \sum\limits_{i=0}^d \alpha_i(E) \frac{m^i}{i!}, \] where $H_1, ... ,H_d \in |L|$ are $E$-regular divisors. Then we define the Gieseker (semi)stbility.
