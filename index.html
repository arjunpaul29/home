About Me

I am a Short-term Visiting Fellow (April 12, 2018 to July 31, 2018) in the School of Mathematics of Tata Institute of Fundamental Research, Mumbai. I have submitted my Ph.D. thesis in the Tata Institute of Fundamental Research on April 2018. My Ph.D. thesis advisor is Prof. Indranil Biswas. I have obtained my B.Sc. (Honours) (2007-2010) and M.Sc. (2010-2012) in Mathematics from Jadavpur University, Kolkata, West Bengal, India.


Research Interests

I am interested in studying algebraic geometry. My research interests includes equivariant principal bundles, connections and curvatures on vector bundles and principal bundles, moduli spaces of semistable vector bundles and principal bundles etc.
