About Me

I am a post-doctoral fellow in the department of Mathematics, IIT Bombay, Mumbai, India. Before that, I was a post-doctoral fellow in Mathematics in the Institute of Mathematical Sciences (IMSc), Chennai, India from August 2018 to February 2019. I did my Ph.D. in Mathematics from the Tata Institute of Fundamental Research, Mumbai, India; my Ph.D. thesis advisor is Prof. Indranil Biswas. I did B.Sc. (Honours) (2007-2010) and M.Sc. Research Interests

I am interested in studying algebraic geometry. My research interests includes equivariant principal bundles, connections and curvatures on vector bundles and principal bundles, moduli spaces of semistable vector bundles and principal bundles etc.
