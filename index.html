About Me

I am a post-doctoral fellow in the department of Mathematics, IMSc, Chennai, India. I did my Ph.D. in Mathematics from the Tata Institute of Fundamental Research, Mumbai, India; my Ph.D. thesis advisor is Prof. Indranil Biswas. I did B.Sc. (Honours) (2007-2010) and M.Sc. (2010-2012) in Mathematics from Jadavpur University, Kolkata, West Bengal, India.


Research Interests

I am interested in studying algebraic geometry. My research interests includes equivariant principal bundles, connections and curvatures on vector bundles and principal bundles, moduli spaces of semistable vector bundles and principal bundles etc.
